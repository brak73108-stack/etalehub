Product Overview

Your AI-powered business operating system.

EtaleHub isn't just another dashboard. It's an intelligent AI assistant that coordinates invoices, schedules reminders, remembers customer histories, and manages your trade admin.

Core Interface

The Command Centre

Instead of navigating complex menus, typing in small forms, and searching for customer accounts, EtaleHub gives you a single natural language input box.

Type or speak what happened. "I serviced Mrs Smith's boiler. She paid £180 by card. Book her annual service." The system safely breaks it down and updates the records instantly.

"Draft a quote for replacing two radiators for Sarah Brown."

Architecture

The Multi-Agent Engine

Behind the single prompt, specialized AI agents cooperate to handle different areas of your business admin.

👤

Office Manager Agent

Coordinates workflows, routes tasks to specialized agents, confirms outcomes, and asks for approvals.

🧾

Invoicing Agent

Drafts professional invoices, records payments, updates status flags, and formats payment reminders.

📅

Scheduler Agent

Manages calendars, reserves bookings, warns about route conflicts, and manages recurring boiler service dates.

Safety First

Human-in-the-Loop Approvals

We don't believe in fully autonomous systems messing up customer communications.

EtaleHub automatically performs safe database actions like completing jobs or updating properties, but requires your explicit green-light before sending emails, quotes, invoices, or reminders.
